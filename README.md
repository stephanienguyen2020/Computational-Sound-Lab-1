# WebAudio Synthesizer Lab 1

## Features
- Play multiple notes at once without clipping.
- Choose between Sine, Square, Sawtooth, and Triangle waves.
- Smooth attack and release to prevent audio clicking.
- Interactive keyboard that lights up when played.
- The background color subtly shifts based on the pitch of the note played.

## How to Play
- **Row 1**: Z X C V B N M (White keys) | S D G H J (Black keys)
- **Row 2**: Q W E R T Y U (White keys) | 2 3 5 6 7 (Black keys)
